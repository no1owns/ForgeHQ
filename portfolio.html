About

I started my creative journey as a designer obsessed with solving problems through visual storytelling. Today I lead high-impact creative strategies for global tech brands, transforming complex product capabilities into compelling narratives.

With 15+ years of experience across UX/UI design, brand strategy, and creative direction, I've had the privilege of shaping campaigns for companies like MongoDB, DocuSign, BetterUp, and AppOmni — as well as cultural institutions that demand storytelling at its finest.

I believe every pixel should carry intent. Every system should scale. Every story should move people.
