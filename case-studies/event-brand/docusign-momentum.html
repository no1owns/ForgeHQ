Overview

DocuSign's Biggest Annual Moment

Momentum is DocuSign's flagship user conference — the event where customers, partners, developers, and executives come together each year. It's the brand's highest-visibility moment: keynotes broadcast to thousands, sessions attended by decision-makers, and an environment that signals DocuSign's place in the enterprise landscape.

I was brought in to lead creative direction for the full conference experience — developing an event identity that felt like a genuine expression of where DocuSign was heading.

Approach

An Event Experience That Builds Trust

Conference creative often defaults to abstract energy — gradients, swooshes, and vague motion graphics. For Momentum, I took a more grounded approach: a visual system built on the idea of agreements in motion, drawing from the real-world contexts where DocuSign lives — real estate closings, employment offers, enterprise contracts.

The identity extended from pre-event email and social campaigns through to on-site signage, stage design, session graphics, app interfaces, and post-event digital content — all connected by a consistent visual thread.
