Overview

Building a World-Class Event Experience

MongoDB World is one of the largest developer conferences in the database space, bringing together thousands of engineers, architects, and technology leaders annually. Over three years, I led the creative direction for the full event experience — from pre-event marketing assets to on-site environmental design and post-event digital content.

The challenge was consistent: create an event identity that felt exciting and fresh year over year while staying coherent with MongoDB's evolving brand.

Approach

A Scalable Creative System for Live Events

Rather than treating each conference as a one-off design project, I built a modular event creative system — one that could flex across formats (keynote slides, booth graphics, digital displays, merchandise) while maintaining a unified visual voice.

Each year's theme was developed in close collaboration with the marketing and product teams to ensure the event narrative aligned with where MongoDB was headed as a company. Product demos were designed as storytelling moments, not just technical showcases.
