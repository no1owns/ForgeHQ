Overview

Giving the Brand a Voice in Every Room

BetterUp helps companies invest in their people's growth through AI-powered coaching — a compelling product with real outcomes. But like many fast-scaling SaaS companies, the gap between the product's value and how that value was communicated in sales decks and content marketing had grown wide.

I worked with BetterUp's content marketing and sales teams to design a presentation system that could close that gap — one that was visually rigorous, easy to maintain, and built to communicate complex ideas simply.

Approach

A System Built to Scale With the Team

The core challenge wasn't visual — it was structural. Sales reps were building their own decks from scratch, content marketers were riffing on inconsistent templates, and the result was a fragmented picture of the brand in the market.

I designed a modular presentation framework: a master template system in Google Slides with a library of interchangeable components — data visualization layouts, testimonial formats, product screenshots, and narrative flow structures. I also collaborated on the messaging strategy, helping teams understand not just what the slides should look like, but what story they should tell and in what order.
