Swedish

Stimulates circulation, releases tight muscles, and promotes relaxation. Swedish massage can reduce anxiety, tension, and help with depression by calming the nervous system.

Therapeutic Massage

Effective for managing chronic pain, reducing stress, and treating specific conditions like sciatica, plantar fasciitis, and frozen shoulder.

Deep Tissue

Breaks down scar tissue and focuses on deeper muscle layers to improve circulation and range of motion with targeted techniques.

Trigger Point

Addresses muscle "knots" and spasms, improving blood flow and relieving chronic pain in specific areas like shoulders and lower back.

Sports Massage

Tailored for athletes to improve flexibility, recovery time, and prevent injuries during physical activities.

Myofascial Massage

Focuses on releasing tension in the myofascial tissues to improve posture, movement, and overall flexibility.

Prenatal

Relieves tension and swelling for expectant mothers, promoting relaxation and comfort during pregnancy.

Reflexology

Applies targeted pressure to reflex points on the feet, hands, and ears to enhance overall well-being and relaxation.
